# AutoArmenia Strategic Alignment

Этот документ определяет долгосрочное стратегическое направление развития платформы.

Все архитектурные и продуктовые решения должны соответствовать этим стратегическим ориентирам.

## Strategic Objectives

### 1. Regional Leadership
Become the most structured and technically consistent automotive marketplace in the region.

### 2. Scalable Marketplace Engine
Build a scalable marketplace engine capable of supporting:
- Private sellers
- Professional dealers
- Commercial and special vehicles
- Future international expansion

### 3. Data Integrity as Competitive Advantage
Prioritize data integrity and structured vehicle classification as competitive advantage.

### 4. Transparent Visibility Logic
Maintain predictable and transparent listing visibility logic.

### 5. Monetization-Ready Architecture
Enable future monetization layers without rewriting core architecture.

### 6. Simple UX Despite Complexity
Keep UX simple despite domain complexity.

### 7. Long-Term Scalability
Design for long-term platform scalability, not short-term feature velocity.

### 8. User Trust Through Determinism
Maintain high user trust through deterministic system behavior.

### 9. No Short-Term Optimization
Avoid architectural decisions that optimize for short-term convenience.

### 10. Strengthening Iterations
Ensure that each iteration strengthens the marketplace engine.

## Strategic Decision Rule

Если изменение не усиливает стратегические цели платформы — оно должно быть пересмотрено или упрощено.
